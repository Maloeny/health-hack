Features

How Is Your Identity Managed?

Admittance Records

Admittance records are securely recorded and analyzed by our Persona Generation Model

Activity Trackers

Data from activity trackers such as your phone or FitBit is fed through our Persona Generation Model

Persona Generation Model

Raw data is fed through our Persona Generation Model to derive actionable insights and create a medical identity

Cryptographic Permissioning

Asymmetric cryptography used to manage accounts and the permissioning of patient data

Distributed Data

Real-time updates and sharing of data through a blockchain-based distributed system

Scalability

Easily expand your network by permissioning new nodes to join the system
